Who We Are

Your Gateway to Visakhapatnam's Wonders

Vizag Travels is a locally rooted, premium tour service dedicated to unveiling the natural splendor, heritage, and hidden treasures of the City of Destiny. From the sun-kissed sands of Rushikonda to the serene, coffee-laden valleys of Araku, we design trips that stick with you forever.

Our fleet of vehicles, handpicked local guides, and custom itineraries guarantee comfort and security while letting you explore at your own pace.
